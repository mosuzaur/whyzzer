App Brief

Whyzzer is a privacy-driven application which helps you gather advice from your friends in a discrete manner. Start using Whyzzer by posting a text-vote when you are in the need of a fast advice or use a photo-vote to be more expressive.

  • Create a new post
  • Write the question you want to ask
  • Choose the answers format: text or photo
  • Provide two options to be voted by your friends
  • Post your question
  • And finnally wait for your friends to choose the correct answer

App Brief

Prove your friends that you can provide a valuable advice for their daily troubles. Their questions will appear on your main screen: the Interest Feed. There, with only just a tap, you can vote between the two answers they proposed. The notification center will make sure that they will not miss the advice/vote you have given.

  • Check your 'Friend's Questions' section on your Interest Feed
  • There you will see questions in wich you were tagged by your friends
  • Vote for the answer you consider to be correct
  • Add a comment if you want to provide additional feedback to your vote
